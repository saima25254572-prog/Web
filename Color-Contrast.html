About WCAG Contrast Standards

The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) define minimum contrast ratios to ensure text is readable for people with visual impairments.

WCAG AA requires a contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1 for normal text and 3:1 for large text (18pt+ or 14pt+ bold).

WCAG AAA requires a contrast ratio of at least 7:1 for normal text and 4.5:1 for large text.

This tool helps you ensure your color combinations meet these important accessibility standards.
