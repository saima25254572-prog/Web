About Color Blindness

Color blindness affects approximately 1 in 12 men and 1 in 200 women worldwide.

Protanopia

Red-blind, difficulty distinguishing between red and green hues.

Deuteranopia

Green-blind, similar to protanopia but with different color confusion.

Tritanopia

Blue-blind, difficulty distinguishing between blue and yellow hues.
