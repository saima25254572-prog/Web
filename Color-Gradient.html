About CSS Gradients

Linear Gradients
Linear gradients transition colors along a straight line. You can specify the direction and multiple color stops.
Radial Gradients
Radial gradients transition colors outward from a center point. You can control the shape and size.
Conic Gradients
Conic gradients transition colors around a center point. Useful for creating pie charts and color wheels.
