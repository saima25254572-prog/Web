Understanding Your BMI

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on your weight in relation to your height. It applies to most adult men and women aged 20 and over.

Underweight: < 18.5
Normal: 18.5 - 24.9
Overweight: 25 - 29.9
Obese: ≥ 30

Note: BMI is a screening tool, not a diagnostic of body fatness or health. Consult a healthcare provider for assessment.
